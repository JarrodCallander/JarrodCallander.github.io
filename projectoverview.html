Project Overview

My project idea is a smartphone app and web application aimed at young adults and young families. The app is a platform where users households or families with split accounts such as share houses will be able to pay bills automatically and hassle free. The app will be advertised as a tool that will make living with friends easy and financially safe. As a biproduct the app will also add security to shopping and paying bills online due to a virtual card number that will be used instead of an actual credit or debit card.
